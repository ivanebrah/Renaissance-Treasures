Mona Lisa by Leonardo da Vinci: This masterpiece features a woman with a serene expression, sitting against a landscape background with winding paths and a distant horizon.

Sistine Madonna by Raphael: It depicts the Madonna holding the Christ Child, flanked by Saint Sixtus and Saint Barbara, with two cherubs at the bottom. This painting is celebrated for its composition and divine representation.

The Assumption of the Virgin by Peter Paul Rubens: It illustrates the Virgin Mary being assumed into heaven, surrounded by angels and cherubs, with figures looking up from below. The dynamic movement and vibrant colors are characteristic of Rubens' style.
