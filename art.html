The Renaissance period was marked by an extraordinary flourishing of art. Artists like Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Raphael created masterpieces that are celebrated for their beauty, technical skill, and innovative use of perspective. Leonardo da Vinci, known for works such as the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper, was a master of the High Renaissance. His art is characterized by a deep understanding of human anatomy, light, and shadow.
