Renaissance Highlights

1452 - Birth of Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo da Vinci, a master of art and science, was born. Known for the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper.

1473 - Copernicus' Heliocentric Theory

Nicolaus Copernicus proposed that the Earth revolves around the Sun, revolutionizing astronomy.

1503 - Completion of the Mona Lisa

Leonardo da Vinci completed the Mona Lisa, one of the most famous paintings in history.

1610 - Galileo's Telescope Discoveries

Galileo Galilei made significant astronomical discoveries with his improved telescope.
